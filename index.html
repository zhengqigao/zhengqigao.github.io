About me

Hi, I am a first year PhD student at MIT EECS, advised by Prof. Duane S. Boning. Previously, I obtained my M.S. and B.E. from School of Microelectronics, Fudan University, where I worked closely with Prof. Jun Tao and Prof. Xin Li (Duke Univ.) in the area of electronic design automation. My current research interests lie mainly in design automation for photonic integrated circuits (PICs) and machine learning. In the summer before coming to MIT, I was very lucky to work with Dr. Ron Rohrer on the adjoint method and Prof. Hang Zhao on multimodal learning.

Check out my original solutional manual for Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning.
